8. Sort in Descending and Find Median
Sort the array in descending order and print the median. If even number of elements, take average of middle two.

Input:
5
3 1 4 1 5

Output:
Descending: 5 4 3 1 1
Median: 3
  
Input:
4
10 20 30 40

Output:
Descending: 40 30 20 10
Median: 25
  
Input:
3
9 8 7

Output:
Descending: 9 8 7
Median: 8
